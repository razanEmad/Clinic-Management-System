About Our Clinic

Our Vision

Since opening our doors nearly 16 years ago, DentCo Clinic has grown into a cornerstone of the financial district, providing specialized, compassionate care across multiple disciplines. While our foundation began with the exemplary Adam Smith, DDS and our dental team, we have expanded to house a full spectrum of health services, including Wellness, Dermatology, and more. We understand that choosing a healthcare provider is a significant decision, and we are honored that you are considering our clinic.

At DentCo Clinic, we are redefining the patient experience in a comprehensive Private Practice setting. Across every department and specialist, you will find a practice where you will:

  • Be Empowered: We involve you actively in all health and treatment decisions, ensuring your choices and voice are respected.
  • Feel Welcomed: Experience a cohesive atmosphere of warmth, respect, and personalized attention from the moment you arrive.
  • Trust Our Expertise: Benefit from the collaborative knowledge of our highly qualified, experienced doctors and specialists who work together for your total well-being.
  • Receive Exceptional Care: We are committed to delivering the highest standards of professional service and the best available treatment across all our specialized departments.
